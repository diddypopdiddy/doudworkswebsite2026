Bio

Meet Vince Doud.

My work sits where teaching, AI literacy, and practical workflow design meet. The goal is not louder AI messaging. It is clearer thinking.

Educator, facilitator, and creator of the AI Production Framework.

Background Video production teacher, instructional technology coach, and arts-program lead.

I started as a video production teacher, moved into instructional technology integration coaching, and am now back in the classroom teaching video production. Outside the classroom, I run arts programs that give students more places to make work, build confidence, and be seen.

Classroom Lens The work starts from real instruction, not abstract tool excitement.

Schools need more than tool demos. They need language, routines, and evidence that learning is still happening when AI enters the process.

  • Teacher-facing sessions stay practical and room-aware.
  • Student-facing framing keeps authorship and process visible.
Recognition Teacher of the Year, Axalta Eagles Pro Teacher, NJ DOE Exemplary Teacher.

Recent school and district contexts have included Pennsville School District and Camden County Educational Services Commission.

How I Work Clear structure, honest language, and useful takeaways over AI theater.

The work is best when it helps a real room move forward: educators get shared language, leaders get clearer questions, and students keep evidence of their thinking.
