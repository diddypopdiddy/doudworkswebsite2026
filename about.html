How I Work Direct conversation, planning, drafting, delivery, and implementation.

You work with me directly. We start with real dialogue about the school, the staff, the students, and the problem in front of you, whether that is in person or over a video call. From there, I help shape the plan, draft the language or materials, prepare the session, and stay focused on what can actually be delivered and implemented.

  • Conversation first: what is happening, what people need, and where AI fits.
