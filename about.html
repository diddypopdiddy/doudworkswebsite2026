Why this workshop model exists

Schools need sequence, not just inspiration.

Too much AI professional learning is either hype-heavy or tool-first. The AI Permit to AI License series was built to give staff a better sequence: understand AI, practice responsible use, choose modes intentionally, and leave with a documented workflow.

The goal is not just exposure. It is staff confidence that can hold up in real classrooms, faculty conversations, and leadership planning.
