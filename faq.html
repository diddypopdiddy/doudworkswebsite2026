FAQ

FAQ.

Common questions about formats, fit, customization, and current delivery.

Who are the workshops for?

Schools, districts, leadership teams, departments, grade-level teams, conferences, and custom education audiences.

What formats are available?

The work can be shaped as the full series, full-day workshops, half-day sessions, 60-90 minute workshops, keynotes, or custom bundles.

Can we start with one session instead of the full series?

Yes. Individual sessions can stand on their own or be combined into a custom sequence that fits your time and goals.

Are sessions in person or virtual?

Current offerings are available in person at limited times. Virtual options for select offerings are coming soon.

What happens after we send a message?

Your inquiry is reviewed for audience, timing, and scope. Vince replies with a best-fit recommendation, then confirms the agenda and next steps if it makes sense to move forward.

Can the content be customized?

Yes. Sessions can be adapted around district goals, subject-area needs, leadership priorities, staff readiness, and the time available.

What do staff leave with?

Staff leave with shared language, better questions, responsible-use habits, and practical workflow structures they can keep using after the session ends.

How do we start?

Use the Contact page and send a short note with the audience, goal, timeline, and any context that matters.
