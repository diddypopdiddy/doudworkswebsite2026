AI Permit

Session 1 · Earn Your AI Permit

The permit stage builds the foundation. Staff learn shared AI literacy, core competencies, responsible classroom expectations, verification habits, and the first AI modes so the team has common language before moving deeper.

  • AI literacy and core competencies
  • Responsible expectations for classroom use
  • Early practice with AI modes and human judgment

AI License

Session 2 · Personal Workflow License

AI License Lab: Build Your Own AI Workflow

Build a defensible AI workflow for organizing, planning, creating, managing tasks, and moving real work forward.

  • AI Modes and Tool Mode for educator and staff work
  • Project-based workflow design
  • Reusable routines that can be refined

Session 3 · Classroom License

AI License Lab: Bring It Into Your Classroom

Bring AI into student-facing work through responsible routines, evidence logs, visible thinking, and the Production Spiral.

  • Classroom expectations and student use cases
  • Evidence Logs and visible learning habits
  • Production Spiral practice with real assignments
