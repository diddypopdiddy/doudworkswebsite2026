Flagship series

AI Permit to AI License professional development for schools.

This is the AI Production Framework learning path for school teams that want staff to move from AI literacy into responsible, documented classroom workflows.

Why teams use it

A structured path for AI literacy and practical classroom workflows.

AI Permit

The permit stage builds the foundation. Staff learn shared AI literacy, core competencies, responsible classroom expectations, verification habits, and the first AI modes so the team has common language before moving deeper.

  • AI literacy and core competencies
  • Responsible expectations for classroom use
  • Early practice with AI modes and human judgment

AI License

The license stage moves staff into stronger use. Teams master the AI modes, apply the production spiral, and turn real use cases into workflows they can explain, test, improve, and make their own.

  • Mode mastery beyond basic prompting
  • Production spiral practice with real classroom tasks
  • Personal workflow design that can be defended and refined
