Workflow training

AI workflow training for educators and school teams.

Move staff from isolated prompts into documented workflows that include planning, production, verification, and reflection steps they can actually reuse.

From prompt to process

The work is built around the production spiral.

Pre-production

Clarify the task, the audience, and where AI should or should not participate.

Production

Use Idea, Learning, Feedback, Tool, and Practice Mode depending on the job.

Post-production

Review the output, strengthen it, and add verification so the work is defensible.

Publish and reflect

Document what worked, what still needs adjustment, and how the workflow should evolve.

What staff leave with

Usable structures, not just a list of prompts.

This training works when a team wants to turn AI exploration into a repeatable process for planning, feedback, communication, or classroom preparation.

It is especially helpful for departments, grade-level teams, or implementation-focused staff learning.

  • Documented classroom and team workflows
  • Verification points built into the process
  • Clearer decisions about where AI fits
  • Less random prompting and more intentional use
